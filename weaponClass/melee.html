Hellsweeper VR is a roguelike VR game set in the realm Lands in Twain that act as its own hell where human souls will gather and warp into different demons depending on their actions when they were alive. The player begins by signing a story based contract that makes them the hellsweeper, the hellsweeper is a being that is given power in order to rid the ever growing population of demons in the Lands in Twain in order to protect the human realm from danger.

There are no confining classes like warrior or ranger that can be found in other games.

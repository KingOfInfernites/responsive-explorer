Sword

The Hellsweeper's sword, a strong and simple weapon that the player can weld. Dispite being the first weapon the player gets, it can hold its own in advanced battles and can take some time to master.

The sword has many abilities to help the player eradicate the demons that stand before them. The first is activated by holding the sword with two hands, when doing so the blade will increase in length shown with the end glowing yellow and a visible end of the original blade being seen. The second is activated when charging the blade with the trigger until the characters on the blade glow red, from here you can swing the sword and let go of trigger to send out a slash of light that will damage enemies. You can also thrust the sword forward, letting go of the trigger during the action to send out a light spike that will also damage enemies.

Infusion

When the sword is infused with Fire magic the blade will inflict fire damage and can ignight enemies. Also, the blade gains an extention and can reach futher.

When the sword is infused with Frost magic the blade will inflict frost damage and can freeze enemies.

When the sword is infused with Volt magic the blade will inflict electric damage and can stun enemies.
